I’m a postdoc (onderzoeker) in the algebraic topology group at the University of Utrecht, where my mentor is Ieke Moerdijk. Before that I was a postdoc at the University of Bonn, where I also obtained my PhD under the supervision of Stefan Schwede in 2021.

My research concerns equivariant and global homotopy theory, with a particular focus on equivariant infinite loop space theory and adjacent topics like (global) equivariant algebraic K-theory and parameterized semiadditivity. You can find an overview about some of the work I did before 2021 in this video I contributed to the Junior Mathematician Research Archive.
